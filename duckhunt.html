Game Controls

To play the game you must use the buttons below the game screen to direct the cannon.

The cannon can move left, right, up or down using the buttons.

When you are ready to shoot a cannon ball hit the FIRE! button.

To win you must hit all 20 of the ducks down with your 30 cannon balls!

To increase or decrease the difficulty of the game, click the increase or decrease difficulty buttons.
Doing this will speed up or slow down the ducks.

If you want to change the lighting click the toggle light button above!

Have fun and keep playing until you win!
